How Test-time Compute Works

The Breakthrough Paradigm

Traditional models generate answers immediately. But reasoning models like o1 and o3 use a fundamentally different approach that's driving genius-level performance:

  • Chain-of-Thought Generation: Models generate extensive internal reasoning steps before answering
  • Self-Verification: Check their own work and refine answers through multiple iterations
  • Compute Scaling: More thinking time = logarithmically better performance (o3 uses 172x compute for top performance)
  • Reinforcement Learning: Trained to maximize reward for correct reasoning, not just next-token prediction
  • Search Over Solutions: Generate multiple solution paths and select the best one

This approach enables AI to tackle novel problems it has never seen before, achieving genius-level reasoning (133 IQ) and approaching human-level abstract reasoning on benchmarks like ARC-AGI (87.5%).
